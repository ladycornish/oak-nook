Stock Road, Galleywood, Essex

Oak Nook is our self contained, one bedroom apartment which is situated on the edge of Galleywood Common, a local nature reserve in the Essex Countryside. Less than a minutes walk from your private front door you can access the ancient racetrack located within 125 acres of Common Land.

It features a cosy living room, a well equipped kitchen with a bistro dining area, including Nespresso coffee machine, electric hob & oven, microwave and fridge/freezer. A smart TV and Alexa Bluetooth speaker can also be found in the living room. Linen and towels are provided for the bedroom & shower room.

With offroad parking for one car, the driveway is covered by CCTV for your peace of mind.
