How To Find Us

Oak Nook is situated close to the M25, just 1 mile from the A12.

If you are using the A12, take the turning for Galleywood at junction 16 and follow the signs to Galleywood at the roundabout. You will begin driving through the lower part of Galleywood Common.

After 1 mile you will reach a crossroad junction with traffic lights, a restaurnat called Lounge India will be on your right and you can see the church diagonally in front of you to the left. Go straight across at the lights and Oak Nook is the 4th and final house on the left hand side, just prior to the Porsche Garage. Please park on the left hand side of the driveway.

If you are coming from the direction of Chelmsford, when you are driving along Stock Road (the B1007), you will go past the Ego Mediterranean Restaurant on your right hand side, then a short distance up the hill there is a Porsche Garage on the right, Oak Nook is immediately after these premises. Please park on the left hand side of the driveway.

The apartment is available after 3:00 p.m. on day of arrival. Accommodation in the apartment is restricted to the persons stated on the booking form.
